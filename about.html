About Me

Let me tell you a little bit about myself...

BIO

I'm a bright, hardworking student that loves to learn new things, meet new people, establish authentic connections, and build cool stuff with technology. Technology has the ability to make people's lives better, and I love being a part of that.

When I'm not in class, playing board games with friends, or programming something new, I'm usually working with the Newman Catholic Ministry at UTD. As President of NCM, I lead a team of 12 officers (13 including me!) in facilitating 8-15 hours of events each week. I firmly believe that a good leader is someone that embraces the intelligence and autonomy of their team and uses the individual strengths of each member to accomplish the common goal, and this is the model I use to lead NCM. Micromanagement is the antithesis of leadership; a good leader inspires their team so that they want to do a good job, and then empowers them so that they can do a good job.
